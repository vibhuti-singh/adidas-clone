WORKOUT CLOTHES, FOR ANY SPORT
adidas designs for and with athletes of all kinds. Creators, who love to change the game. Challenge conventions. Break the rules and define new ones. Then break them again. We supply teams and individuals with athletic clothing pre-match. To stay focussed. We design sports apparel that get you to the finish line. To win the match. We support women, with bras and tights made for purpose. From low to high support. Maximum comfort. We design, innovate and itterate. Testing new technologies in action.
